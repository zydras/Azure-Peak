# Spellblade & Spellfist Design Doc 
It is functionally an anime class and I am sick of hiding it.

## Arcyne Momentum
The mechanics that hold it all together. 

It works when parried / dodged so that they are rewarded for attacking. 

Exceptionally important considering that 90% parry / dodge chance is considered the norm. 

It forces them to weave in melee and actually do some Spellbladery instead of Knockdown spell into bladery.

Taking damage while built up for Ult prevents them from holding it forever to zone their opponent - they WILL take attritional damage with such scummy tactic.

Spellblade in particular has slightly lower stats and must compensate. They also lack most armor / dodge based clutch. 

Spellfist currently have higher stats on par. We'll see if it needs adjustment.

## Abilities
3 + 1 Abilities, easy to rotate, cool chants.

Ultimate power gated behind building up stacks so that it can feel impactful without being spammed out in combat. It allows me to assign a greater budget

By design all Spellfist / Spellblade has a way to escape grapples or being dorpeled (surrounded). I hate these two methods of winning combat, therefore they are by design good against these two. 

You can still left click them to death.
